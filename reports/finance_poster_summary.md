# Finance Poster Summary

Question: Do judgment-intensive finance occupations have a higher mean projected growth than process-driven finance occupations?

Statistical test:
- Test: one-sided Welch two-sample t-test
- H0: the mean projected growth is the same for judgment-intensive and process-driven finance occupations
- Ha: judgment-intensive finance occupations have a higher mean projected growth

Results:
- t = 1.916
- df = 9.600
- p-value = 0.042821
- decision = reject H0

Conclusion: The finance occupations we labeled as judgment-intensive show a significantly higher mean projected growth than the process-driven group.
