# Projected Growth T-Test Results

Test: Welch two-sample t-test (one-sided)

Hypotheses:
- mu_1 = mean projected growth for judgment-intensive finance occupations
- mu_2 = mean projected growth for process-driven finance occupations
- H0: mu_1 - mu_2 = 0
- Ha: mu_1 - mu_2 > 0

- n_1 = 7
- n_2 = 5
- mean_1 = 5.714
- mean_2 = -0.600
- t = 1.916
- df = 9.600
- p-value = 0.042821
- alpha = 0.05
- decision = reject H0

Conclusion: There is convincing evidence that judgment-intensive finance occupations have a higher mean projected growth than process-driven finance occupations.
